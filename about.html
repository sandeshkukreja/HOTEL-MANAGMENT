About Our Hotel




"Gold Coast Hotel is located in the heart of the historic center of Gold Coast in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of parks, buildings, shops and monuments.
Gold Coast Hotel is part of a lovingly restored 1800 palace.
On entering this Gold Coast Hotel , you will immediately sense its special intimate atmosphere that makes you feel like being in your own home. Each detail has been passionately chosen and each room deserves a visit. "

WHY CHOOSE US?

The rooms have been recently refurbished and are fully ensuite with toilet, shower and bath and toileteries. Also available in the room is Tea and Coffee making Facilities, Flat Screen TV, Hairdryer and Free Wifi. Iron and ironing board available upon request.

Our main goal is to let you live an exciting and unforgettable holiday creating customized itineraries according to your preferences. We are glad to create with our guests friendly relationship and to share smiles and small talks.

The Hotel has fine venues for meetings/conferences, private parties, banquets and corporate functions. Our conference halls cater for up to ; 100 guests.
